I'm currently a PhD student at Shenzhen International Graduate School, Tsinghua University (SIGS, THU) in Shenzhen. My supervisor is Prof. Zhiyong Wu. Before that, I received my bachelor’s degree from Sichuan University. I have previously participated in the development of music and speech generative models, including SkyMusic.
My current research interests include Speech Synthesis, Music Generation, 3D Dance Generation, Singing Voice Synthesis, etc.
